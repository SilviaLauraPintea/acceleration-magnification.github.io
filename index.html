Yichao Zhang, Silvia L. Pintea, Jan C. van Gemert

Video Acceleration Magnification

This work focuses on magnifying temporal changes in videos. Specifically, we ignore linear motion and propose to magnify acceleration: non-linear temporal changes. We do so in an Eulerian fashion, without optical flow estimation, temporal alignment or region annotations.
